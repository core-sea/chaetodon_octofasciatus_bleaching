# *Chaetodon octofasciatus* bleaching dataset

## Overview
This repository contains the datasets supporting the manuscript:

> **Tracking coral bleaching and *Chaetodon octofasciatus* dynamics in the Gulf of Thailand during the fourth global mass bleaching event**

Elba Festo <sup>1,2</sup>, Victoria J. Fahey <sup>2</sup> & Cristina Gioia Di Camillo <sup>1</sup>

1. Department of Life and Environmental Sciences, Marche Polytechnic University via Brecce Bianche 60131 Ancona, Italy
2. Center for Oceanic Research & Education (COREsea), Chaloklum, Koh Phangan, Thailand

The data were collected from coral reef surveys conducted around **Koh Phangan (Gulf of Thailand)** during the 2024 global mass coral bleaching event and associated pre- and post-bleaching periods.

This repository is **data-only** and does **not** contain analysis code.

## Data description
The datasets include:

### *Coral condition*
- Bleaching prevalence `COREsea_LTMP_bleaching_data.csv`
- Live coral cover (LCC) `COREsea_LTMP_LCC_data.csv`
- Coral composition `COREsea_LTMP_LCC_composition_data.csv`

### *Butterflyfish abundance and demographics*
- Adult and juvenile *C. octofasciatus* abundance `COREsea_8banded_abundance_data.csv`
- Individual size & mass `COREsea_8banded_ind_mass_data.csv`

### *Juvenile settlement patterns*
`COREsea_juvenile_settlement_data.csv`
- Settlement occurrence relative to coral host genus 
- Settlement relative to coral bleaching status

---

## Data collection
All data were collected using standard **underwater visual census** and **benthic survey** methods conducted by trained observers. Surveys were performed across multiple reef sites and reef zones around Koh Phangan.

Full methodological details are provided in the **Methods** section of the associated manuscript.
